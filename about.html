Hey! My name is Franchesca Bethel, MBA

“The past is your lesson. The present is your gift. The future is your motivation.”

— Zig Ziglar
B.S. Fashion Merchandising & Retailing MBA, Operations & Supply Chain

Franchesca Bethel is a passionate and driven marketing professional and entrepreneur, holding a B.S. in Fashion Merchandising and Retailing and an MBA in Operations and Supply Chain Management. With over five years of marketing and leadership experience, Franchesca has honed her ability to lead, inspire, and guide women and peers alike. Her extensive background in fashion and business, combined with her dedication to empowering others, uniquely positions her to create impactful experiences that nurture self-confidence, independence, and community growth.

As a scholarly marketing professional, Franchesca views her career not only as a job but as a calling. She believes in balancing her life in alignment with her educational values and professional goals, seeking to uplift those around her. Franchesca is committed to fostering a community that embraces life's challenges with an open mind and innovative solutions. She sees it as her duty to advocate for women's rights, creating safe spaces that offer meaningful opportunities for personal growth, positive self-image, and the strength to make healthy choices that last a lifetime.

Franchesca considers herself a “Direction Setter,” the type of leader who dreams of a future entirely outside of the present experience. “A Direction Setter can see a picture of a desired future and is willing to do what is required to get there” (Phillips, 2015).
